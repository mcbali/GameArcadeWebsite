Tic Tac Toe

Tic-tac-toe is a classic paper-and-pencil game played between two opponents on a grid of 3x3 squares. How to Play: Players take turns marking an empty square with their symbols: "X" for one player and "O" for the other, until one player achieves a row of three of their symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, or until all squares are filled without a winner, resulting in a draw. Mark an empty square by clicking!

Memory Match

Memory Match is a simple game where you challenge your memorization skills with different levels of varying difficulty. How To Play: Click two squares per turn to reveal the hidden fruits. If you successfully match a pair, you can keep the fruit! Win by matching all pairs of fruit. Compete yourself by trying to win in as fast of a time that you can accomplish!.

Snake

Snake is a classic retro arcade game where you must slither towards the apples to grow and increase your score! Avoid hitting the walls and eating your own tail. How to Play: Use wasd keys to control the direction of your snake's movement.

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is a simple yet addictive game where the objective is to achieve the highest score possible by surviving as long as possible by remaining airborne without hitting any obstacles. How to Play: Click to flap upwards.
